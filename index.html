About Us

Mini World: CREATA is a free-to-play sandbox game launched in 2017 and now enjoys hundreds of millions of registered players in over 30 countries. As the name suggests, creation is the core value of the game, where players can create everything in the meta-universe by using the objects and tools provided. Quality instant games by our players, adventures, diverse structures, and customized mechanisms are all part of creation. We are passionate about building an inclusive ecosystem with a myriad of user-generated content and connecting people through playing.
